What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI that enables computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. NLP powers chatbots, virtual assistants, sentiment analysis, text classification, and moreâ€”making technology more accessible and interactive for everyone.

My NLP Process & Best Practices

  1. Problem Definition: Understand the business need and define clear NLP objectives.
  2. Data Collection & Cleaning: Gather and preprocess text data for quality results.
  3. Text Analysis: Tokenization, stemming, lemmatization, and feature extraction.
  4. Model Selection: Use state-of-the-art models (transformers, RNNs, etc.) or cloud NLP APIs.
  5. Chatbot Development: Build conversational agents using Dialogflow, LangChain, and custom Python/JS code.
  6. Testing & Evaluation: Ensure accuracy, relevance, and user-friendliness.
  7. Deployment: Integrate NLP solutions into web apps, chatbots, or business workflows.
  8. Continuous Improvement: Monitor, retrain, and update models for evolving needs.
