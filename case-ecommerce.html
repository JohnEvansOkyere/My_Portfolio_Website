E-commerce Sales Uplift

Problem: Stagnant sales and high churn in a competitive market.

Process

  • Analyzed historical transaction data, customer segments, and product affinities.
  • Developed predictive churn models; visualized results in Tableau.
  • Deployed recommendations engine via Streamlit app for real-time insights.

Solution

Created a dashboard showing actionable sales KPIs, customer retention metrics, and "next best offer" for each segment.

Outcome & Impact

  • Monthly sales increased by 28% over 3 months.
  • Churn rate reduced from 14.2% to 8.5%.
“Evans’ data insights transformed how we think about our customers. The dashboard is part of every Monday meeting now!”
— E-commerce Startup CEO
